    Alacourt users have
    Role-Based Access Control

    (RBAC)

    User

    Users have access to Alacourt , however, does not allow access to the Administrator functions. Users cannot add, edit, delete users or change billing information or the ability to setup Image Packages. They can update their information and use Alacourt in the same way as an Alacourt Administrator.

    Learn more
    Administrator

    An Italic Text Administrator is a user with additional permissions.
    Administrator can add, edit, delete users. Unlike users, Administrator have access to the billing information and the ability to setup Image Packages. Administrator use Alacourt in the same way as an Alacourt User.

    User FAQ's

    Forgot my password                              

    To reset your password, please ensure that the email address in your account is valid and current. A temporary password will be emailed to you. You will be prompted to change your password the next time you log into the system. Go to "Forgot Password" now.

     How can a I update my User Name?

    Begin by logging into your alacourt account.

    Update/Change my Password  

    Begin by logging into your alacourt account.
    Go to the bottom left of the screen and select "Update User Info". Next, go to the top box in the middle of the screen where it says "Change Password". Next, Type your current password in the top box. Below, type your new password and then retype the new password in the bottom box. Remember the Password must be a combination of letters and numbers and a minimum of 8 characters in length.

    Once complete, select the "Update Password" Button.
